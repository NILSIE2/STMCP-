STMCP's Important Announcement!

  • In line with STMCP’s commitment to innovation and digital transformation, we are thrilled to announce the development of our very own Digital Platform. This upcoming system aims to enhance efficiency, accessibility, and communication for all members and administrators. Here’s what to look forward to:
  • Online Membership Application: Seamless and convenient registration process—no more manual forms or long waiting times.
  • Digital Membership List: A secure and organized list of all registered members, easily accessible for monitoring and reference.
  • Administrator and Moderator Accounts: Exclusive accounts for authorized personnel to manage user data, approve membership requests, and oversee system operations.
  • Secure Login System: Each user will have a personal account with encrypted login credentials to ensure data protection and user privacy.
  • Automated Notifications and Announcements: Real-time updates and reminders will be sent directly through the platform to keep all members informed.
