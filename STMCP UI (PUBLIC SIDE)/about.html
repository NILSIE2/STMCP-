Our History

The journey of Star Touring Motorcycle Club Philippines (STMCP)—from humble beginnings to a strong community committed to charity and riding adventures.

Foundation

  • The Star Touring Motorcycle Club Philippines (STMCP) was founded by four key individuals, affectionately known within the club as the "4 Pillars"—Erick Lee, Alo Yap, Ericson Gallito, and Mike Miranda.
  • On July 5, 2021, STMCP was officially registered with the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC), marking its formal recognition as an organization.

Growth & Expansion

  • Since its inception, STMCP has steadily grown, attracting motorcycle enthusiasts from the Luzon region and beyond.
  • The club has expanded through word of mouth and active community involvement, creating a network of passionate riders.
  • Today, STMCP is recognized not only as a riding club but as a vibrant and growing community united by shared values and advocacy.

Advocacy & Events

  • STMCP is deeply committed to giving back to the community. It organizes charity rides and outreach programs to help those in need.
  • The club fosters brotherhood, adventure, and social responsibility among its members through its advocacy-driven activities.
  • To become an official member, applicants must meet the following requirements:
    • Attend one Eyeball (EB) gathering
    • Participate in one charity event

Current Status & Future Outlook

  • As of today, STMCP boasts an estimated 1,500 members and continues to expand across regions.
  • The club is expected to continue its legacy of uniting motorcycle enthusiasts while making a lasting positive impact on society.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to build a community among all riders and make a Big Star Family that have Good Camaraderie among its members. We also promote touring all over the Philippines and Experience the Ride that you’ll never forget. STMCP and its Local Chapters also give Charities. We’ll support and participate in different motorcycle events and activities to pursue, promote and protect the interests of motorcyclists; to develop better and more enthusiastic relations between the general public. STMCP promotes motorcycle and road safety for the welfare of the entire STMCP family.

Our Vision

Our long-term goal: Create Members for Life. We will earn our members’ loyalty. We will listen to them, anticipate their needs, and act to create value in their eyes. We want to be known for our reliability, flexibility, and responsiveness. Growth, longevity and member satisfaction will naturally follow. We will empower our members in an enriching environment of trust, cooperation and mutual respect.

Core Values

S - Sharing

One of the main advocacies of STAR is sharing what we can to the needy. We also promote selfless riders that always think not just of him/herself but also the welfare of the others. Share the road, share the load. We will travel with a purpose (Leisure, Charities and Social Activities like tree planting, clean up drive etc.). Time will be well spent. We will also make sure that we will have time to share our weekends with our Families and loved ones so we won’t have weekly activities. Our activities will be limited to Bi-Weekly to give the members time to save and prepare.

  • Charity Events
  • Community Engagement
  • Family Time

T - Transparency

The club promotes truthfulness to its members and aspirants. This includes financial, personal, and social activities of the club. STAR and its leadership (P4 or the 4 Pillars) will have its co-equal level of decision-making to ensure that a certain individual including the President, the Chairman of the Board, the Board Members, and other National Executive Leaders will not promote personal gains and interest in the club.

  • Clear Financials
  • Open Decision Making
  • Honest Leadership

A - Accountability

We highly promote accountability on what we do. We brag and share things such as projects and activities to promote the Club and its accomplishments. We’ll also make sure that official members and aspirants will be held accountable for any wrongdoings in the club including all the National, Regional, and Local Leaders from top to bottom. Certain penalties like suspension from participating in our official activities up to expulsion from the club will be merited for any member/s and aspiring member/s for any violation of the club’s rules and regulations.

  • Project Sharing
  • Responsibility for Actions
  • Consequences for Violations

R - Respect

It will be our everyday practice. Everyone deserves to be respected. Respect not just to its members and officers but also to our co-riders, co-road users, respect to nature, and respect to the law of the land and our club by-laws. Respect is the foundation of greatness.

  • Respect for Each Other
  • Respect for the Environment
  • Respect for the Law
